Here's a tag guideline:

- striking: punch, kick, elbow, knee, headbutt, stomp, open-hand
- wrestling: clinching, takedown, throw, pin, sweep
- grappling: choke, lock, slicer, position
- defense: guard, footwork, escape, turnover, head-movement, block
